Mission

INGOMA SAFARIS' mission is to showcase to the local community and visitors the hidden historical masterpieces of the country and those of the East-African community.

With the understanding that every tourist is unique and that the discovery of a new territory is an exceptional moment in everyone's life, INGOMA SAFARIS will strive to give you the opportunity to live those unique moments and share your emotions as you discover new sites⋅
