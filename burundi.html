Burundi

  • Capitale : Bujumbura
  • Governement : Republic
  • Currency : burundian francs
  • Languages : French, Kirundi
  • Population : 8 575 172 (2011)

Located in the heart of Africa, BURUNDI, the country of a thousand hills is a home of amazing treasures as well as tourist spots such as parks, beaches, royal residences, waterfalls etc... It is a real pleasure and a feast for the eyes to behold the natural beauty of this beautiful land of milk and honey as burundian wisdom dictates.

With a pastoral and agricultural landscape with many tea plantations in the east, Burundi has a grassy savannah vegetation and trees where eucalyptus, acacia and palm oil bloom. Burundi is a country with an exceptional natural wealth and amazing culture including the famous sacred drums. Oftenly, unthought destination, Burundi is a country that has much to offer: his mythical Lake Tanganyika, splendid birds' lake, its hills and valleys, the wild savannah, forests and especially its enigmatic fabulous and warm welcoming from the locals. Burundi was colonized by Germans and Belgians. It became independent on 1 July 1962 and its national hero, Prince Louis RWAGASORE, was murdered October 13, 1961.

Entry requirements

A visa is required for all foreigners entering Burundi, except for nationals of the East African Community.

To get a visa, you must

  • fill out a form at the Embassy of Burundi, indicating the type of visa requested (tourism, business, etc..)
  • provide an international certificate of vaccination against yellow fever,
  • a plane ticket, a valid passport(for six months after return date),
  • an invitation from a resident or a hotel booking,
  • two photos passport and 60 euros in cash.

All nationalities pay a fee for a residence visa except for citizens of the East African Community (Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda and Tanzania) for which entry is free. Visas are available on arrival at the airport or border.

  • The entry visa is U.S. $ 90 per month
  • The transit visa is U.S. $ 40 for three days
  • The settlement visa is U.S. $ 500 for 2 years
  • The tourist visa is U.S. $ 2 per day
  • The visa entry and exit is U.S. $ 70 per month
  • Permanent residence visa is U.S. $ 600.
