Touring Services

INGOMA SAFARIS offers guided tours of Burundi and some east-african countries (Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda and Tanzania). Different package tours are available with a possibility of having a fully customized tour.

Ingoma Safaris, in partnership with BAP - Business Applications Provider, is also specialising in private and professional event organization, with ushering services and an optional web and social media exposure/advertising.
